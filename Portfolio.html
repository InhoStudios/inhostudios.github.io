inhostudios Logo Design and Branding


For my own personal website and development, I made a set of logos and colour palettes. The font used in the text is Kollektif, and the logo is supposed to embody an artsy feeling, while keeping those hard edges and corners. I chose to use pastel and pale colours to bring a more soft, welcoming feel. Original logos used a much harsher, highlighter blue and yellow colour pattern, which was a lot more jarring and hard to look at. The slight offset of logo design itself is an anagram on my initials, ASZ. The Z is typically the most obvious, but the A and S also exists to some extent in the right side. My original design tried more to show the letters, but a more abstract approach seemed to look better.

Vikings Dev Logo


The Vikings Dev logo was designed for Burnaby North's Coding Club. It was designed with a very simple goal in mind: incorporate our schools mascot into a coding environment. Since our club is called "Vikings Dev," I decided to draw a Viking head in a pair of braces. I decided to add a stroke to the logo to make it look more friendly and cartoon-like, but the logo also exists in a stroke-less state for certain use cases.
