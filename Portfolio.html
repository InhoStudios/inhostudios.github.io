inhostudios Logo Design and Branding



For my own personal website and development, I made this set of logos and colour palettes. The font used in the text is Kollektif, and the logo is supposed to embody an artsy feeling, while keeping those hard edges and corners. The colours picked were pastel and pale colours to bring a more soft, welcoming feel. Original logos used a much harsher, highlighter blue and yellow colour pattern, which was a lot more jarring and hard to look at.
