Global Gratitude


Global gratitude is a bot designed to spread positivity and positive messages. It takes good messages and vibes and posts them on twitter automatically, as well as being accessible from it's website. It is still in it's early stages of development(mostly planning) but it is planned to use machine learning to parse and categorize the messages.

The Road


The road is a platformer / top-down adventure game based on the novel by Cormac McCarthy. It's staged in a post apocalyptic world, where you, the player, have one goal: reach the southern coast and escape the winter. And above all else, keep your son alive.

A Lone Oak


A simple single player 2D survival game. A Lone Oak uses random terrain generation to create a randomized side-scrolling level for the player to explore. The player obtains resources from a single oak tree that resides in the centre of the world, and must survive until rescue arrives.
