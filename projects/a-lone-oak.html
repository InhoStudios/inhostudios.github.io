Current Progress

January 11, 2018

A Lone Oak has been in development for quite a long time. The premise of the game is that you're thrown into a barren winter landscape, with nothing but a single oak tree and some plants and shrubs to survive. Zombies, wolves, and pissed off bears spawn at night to combat you, and the weather poses a fearful adversary as well. There's still a lot of bugs to fix and things to do, however.
