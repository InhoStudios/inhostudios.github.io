The Road is a book by Cormac McCarthy, and it's really quite interesting. It runs on the premise of a man and his son in a post apocalyptic world, fleeing the cold of winter by travelling south, and avoiding bands of rogues and gangs that live along The Road. It's a really amazing read, and I've been inspired to make a game out of it (I'm not sure if it already exists but it's a personal non-commercial project anyways, at least for now)
