January 24, 2018

The VHHacks website is part of a greater High-school Hackathon initiative. I have the hope of bringing accessible and effective coding events to students all over the lower-mainland, and hopefully soon even all over Canada. Of course, there is much work to be done, but I have high hopes for VHHacks. The first official VHHacks Online Hackathon is on March 16-17, 2019. Registration will start soon on the website.
Sidenote: You might notice that this page is slightly different than other project pages. That's because this is more than just a programming endeavour, and therefore requires more than just a development log. It's a little bit different but it should be no big deal.
