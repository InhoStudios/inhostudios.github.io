The Design: Connect-ed



Final Icon


This serves as the final Connect-ed icon, chosen for simplicity, balanced colours, and a built in emblem. It uses the Kollektif typeface, and uses the puzzle piece motif for multiple symbolic and meaningful purposes. Coincidentally, this was a slight variation of the very first digitized version of the Collect-ed logo, with simply a larger gap to fit in the "ct" from Connect. Other prior designs exist in my notebook, but for the sake of simplicity, this was the original.





Four Variations on a Design Logo


This version was a bit of an experiment, using a blockier puzzle piece and a longer puzzle in general. I noticed that the circle shapes in the letters of Connect-ed could fit quite well inside the puzzle pieces, so I decided to try to fit the O in the notch. It doesn't look quite elegant, and probably isn't one of my finest works.





Staccato: Variation II


Once again, I tried to fit some circular letters into the gaps of the puzzle pieces. It looks a little more connected comopared to the previous rendition, but it could still use some work. Very clashing.





"The Sandwich"


The puzzle piece is definitely the meat of this logo. This logo uses shapes and spacing, and sandwiches the puzzle emblem in between the text, incorporating the shape into it's design. It's nutritious and delicious!





Disharmonious: Variation IV


This was the final experimental design I created with the puzzle motif. It uses the notch of the puzzle piece to represent the C, and although the concept is good, it feels very unbalanced. The text is very heavily skewed to the right side, and overall it's not very harmonious.
