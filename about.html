about me

 

 

who the hell am i?

I’m Andy, otherwise known as inho, and I’m currently a first-year university student pursuing a degree and career in computer science. I also enjoy photography and digital arts, and I love occasionally playing the piano (I’m too lazy to play it often). You can see some of my photos on my Instagram account, @airborneicarus (or in my gallery page). It’s all pretty beginner, but it’s fun and it’s a nice archive of my life for those nostalgia days. I live in and love Vancouver, Canada. This city is one of the best in the world. I’m not biased or anything, I’m just saying.

Since the spring of 2013, I’ve been pursuing computer programming as both a hobby and a career path. It started as a hobby - I used to make small games for fun, and I experimented with different things to show my friends in school - but it has now completely engulfed my life. My extracurricular pursuits focus on programming, my courses are computer centric, and I’m known as “the technology guy” by most of my friends. It’s become a part of my identity, and I wouldn’t have it any other way.
