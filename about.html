About inhostudios


Hi! I'm inho, currently a high-school student with a huge passion for computer science, programming, graphics design, and hardware.

I started my interest in computers in the spring of 2013. I attended a local spring break camp and learned the basics of coding logic. From there, I began experimenting on my own. Now, I am fluent in GML, Java, and am fairly proficient in C#. Right now I'm experimenting with web design, using HTML and CSS.

Graphics design is another one of my interests. This passion began in school, where I began designing logos, artwork, and all sorts of different things for projects at school, clubs, or personal passion projects. You can find some of my work in the 'Portfolio' tab.
As a student, I find it very enjoyable to be able to pursue my passions, and do things I'm interested in doing. If anything I do interests you, feel free to contact me with the contact form below.
